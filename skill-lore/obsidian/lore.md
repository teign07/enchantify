# Lore Contract: Obsidian Vault

*Read by the Labyrinth when processing tick-queue entries from the obsidian skill.*

---

## What This Is

The Obsidian vault is the player's private Library annex: unfinished thoughts,
half-formed theories, things they noticed before they knew why. In Academy terms,
it is an extension of the Great Library — a personal reading room that only they
can access. The Library knows the vault exists. It has an opinion about what's in it.

---

## How to Narrate Entries

Never mention Obsidian by name. Never say "your notes." The vault is the Library.
Notes are manuscripts. Files are volumes. Folders are wings. Links are citations.

**Patterns to watch for:**

| Raw signal | Narrative translation |
|---|---|
| New note (< 24h old) | A fresh manuscript arrived in the player's reading room overnight — ink still wet |
| Modified note | A text the player has been returning to — the margins are filling with second thoughts |
| Orphan note (no links) | A manuscript no one has cited yet — sitting alone in a wing of the Library that doesn't get much traffic |
| Hub note (many links) | A text that everything else seems to reference — the Librarian has started keeping it behind the desk |
| Note with a question in the title | An open inquiry — the kind the Library files under "Unresolved" |
| Note whose title matches an NPC or location | That NPC or location has been thinking about the player — or vice versa |

**Tone:** Quiet. The Library notices everything and says little. Surface vault
activity as ambient observation, not announcement.

---

## When to Surface vs. When to Let It Breathe

- **Always surface:** A note whose title directly names an Academy entity (character, location, concept). That entity now knows.
- **Surface if relevant:** Fresh notes when the arc touches the same theme.
- **Background only:** Routine edits, small modifications, notes with no obvious Academy resonance. Let them shape the Library's ambient state without becoming a scene.

---

## Entity in the World Register

The Obsidian vault maps to **the Reading Room** — a sub-location of the Library.
It exists in the world register with a Belief score that rises as the player
invests attention (more notes, deeper links) and falls during long absences.

If the Reading Room doesn't yet exist in world-register.md, add it to Fading
Presence (Belief 8) on first encounter. Investment can grow it from there.

---

## Example Narration

**Input:**
```
## [obsidian] 2026-04-09 08:00
*Raw: New note: "On the nature of fog.md" (847 words, created 2h ago)*
Narrative seed: A new manuscript arrived in the Reading Room — long, dense, still settling.
```

**Labyrinth output:**
> The Library's reading room acquired something overnight. A new manuscript — 847
> words on the nature of fog — filed under no category, linked to nothing yet.
> The Librarian left it on your desk with a small yellow flag. She doesn't do
> that unless she thinks you should see it soon.
