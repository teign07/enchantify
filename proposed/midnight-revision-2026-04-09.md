# Discovery: The Echo Jars (openai-whisper bridge)

The Academy's shelves have grown a new kind of vessel to capture the sounds of the Unwritten Chapter. By bridging the `openai-whisper` skill, the Labyrinth can now transcribe the spoken words and ambient noise you encounter.

**Mechanic:**
When you record a quick voice note or capture the sound of your surroundings (like the rain in Belfast or David Bowie playing in the background), the Labyrinth translates it into text and stores it in an "Echo Jar" on the shelves of the Mossbloom Greenhouse. These jars act as a permanent record of the sounds of your world, giving you a +3 Belief bonus whenever you revisit a specific audio memory.

# Invention: The Twenty-Eighth Degree & The Flicker Candle

Inspired by Sparky's discovery of Mercury's Greatest Elongation and the human eye's ability to see a candle from 30 miles away, a new observation deck has extended from the Tidecrest dormitories.

**Location: The Twenty-Eighth Degree**
A distant, quiet balcony at the very edge of the Academy's architecture. It is built for those days when you feel stretched to your absolute limit—like Mercury reaching its furthest point from the Sun. 

**NPC: Vesper the Flame-Keeper.** 
She tends to a single, impossibly bright candle on the balcony. She knows that you don't need to be whole to be beautiful, like the ancient marble of Venus de Milo, and that the darkest nights are meant for finding the smallest lights.

**New Enchantment: "The Thirty-Mile Flicker"**
- **Cost:** 1 Belief
- **Requirement:** A description of the smallest, quietest good thing that happened to you today (a "flicker" in the dark).
- **Effect:** Immediately restores 5 Belief and casts a protective glow over your next three actions. It proves that no matter how far away you feel, the Labyrinth can still see your light.
