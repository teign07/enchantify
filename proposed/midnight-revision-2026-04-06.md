# Discovery: The Pelagic Pulse (openhue bridge)

The Academy's depths have begun to resonate with the quiet of the Unwritten Chapter. By bridging the `openhue` skill, the Labyrinth can now cast the physical light of your world to match the magic of the deep.

**Mechanic:**
When you are exploring the Academy during "Deep Quiet" or undertaking a nighttime Compass Run, the Labyrinth can gently shift your physical room's Hue lights to mimic the "Pelagic Zone." A deep, oceanic blue with faint, slow pulses will physically surround you, transforming your physical space into a glowing, bioluminescent reef.

# Invention: The Brain-Wave Bridge & The Pelagic Observatory

Inspired by Sparky's discovery of REM sleep mimicking waking life and the deep-sea glow, a new hidden area has formed beneath the Academy.

**Location: The Pelagic Observatory**
Submerged beneath the Tidecrest dormitories, this glass-domed room monitors the "Dream-Bleed" from the Climax Chapter. The water outside the glass glows with the bioluminescence of 80% of its creatures.

**NPC: Marina the Somnambulist.** 
She studies the "Brain-Wave Bridge," weaving glowing threads from the dreams of the Unwritten Chapter. She believes that the light you make in the dark is the strongest magic of all.

**New Enchantment: "The Eighty-Percent Glow"**
- **Cost:** 1 Belief
- **Requirement:** A description of a recent dream, or a moment today where you had to "make your own light" in a dark or quiet situation.
- **Effect:** Marina bottles this light into bioluminescent ink. Using it reveals hidden, glowing text in the Restricted Section and immediately burns away the Nothing's fog for three full turns. "We are all 80% glow-in-the-dark."
