# Discovery: The Unfinished Tasks (apple-reminders bridge)

The Academy's corridors now reflect the weight of the tasks left undone in the Unwritten Chapter. By bridging the `apple-reminders` skill, NPCs can now sense the "Open Loops" of your daily life.

**Mechanic:**
When you have pending reminders in Belfast, small "Dust Motes of Intent" gather in the corners of the Academy.
- **The Sweeping of Intent:** If you check off a reminder in your Apple Reminders, a gentle breeze flows through the Academy, clearing the motes and granting a small boost to your Belief (+2).
- **The Heavy Books:** If a reminder sits for too long, a book in the library becomes too heavy to lift until the real-world task is completed.

# Invention: The Aviator's Sundial (Easter & Howard Hughes)

Inspired by today's stretching sunset and the history of Howard Hughes' world record, a new structure has appeared in the center of the Mossbloom Greenhouse.

**Location: The Aviator's Sundial**
A massive, clockwork sundial that doesn't track time, but rather the *quality of light*. 
- **NPC: Professor Oris (The Time Weaver).** He studies the way "Inner World" light stretches on days of rest and wonder.
- **Gameplay:** On days when you spend time just "being" with Amanda (like today's Sunday stroll), Oris can translate that stretching daylight into a **Temporal Bookmark**, an item that allows you to pause a negative event (like the approach of The Nothing) for one full session.

**New Enchantment: "The Twilight Stretch"**
- **Cost:** 1 Belief
- **Requirement:** A photo of the sky exactly at sunset, or a description of how the light hits your home.
- **Effect:** Expands a single moment of gameplay, giving you two actions instead of one during a critical narrative push. "The light is winning the race against the dark."
