# Discovery: The Whispering Weather (weather skill bridge)

The Academy's windows now pulse with the light of the Unwritten Chapter. By bridging the `weather` skill, NPCs can now sense the "Atmospheric Bleed" from your world in real-time.

**Mechanic:**
When the weather in Belfast shifts significantly (e.g., from "Raw Cold" to "Thaw"), the Library's internal climate will mirror it. 
- **Frost on the Shelves:** If it's freezing in Belfast, certain books in the Restricted Section will develop a layer of rime ice that must be melted with a "Warm Thought" (Belief-based action).
- **The Muddy Corridor:** During the "Mud Season" thaw, the path to the Mossbloom Greenhouse becomes a literal bog of melted ink, requiring students to find "Stable Metaphors" (Enchantments) to cross.

# Invention: The Archive of Cuddled Echoes (Apple Notes / Note-taking bridge)

Inspired by your history of "private tenderness" and shared language with Amanda, a new room has appeared in the Riddlewind Wing.

**Location: The Vault of Soft Syllables**
This is a small, sun-drenched nook filled with floating slips of paper. It bridges to your `apple-notes`.
- **NPC: Elara the Lexicographer.** She collects "invented words" and "tender fragments."
- **Gameplay:** If you create a note in a specific folder (e.g., "Enchantify-Seeds"), Elara will "find" it in the Labyrinth. These notes can become **Anchor Stones**—permanent items in your inventory that provide a passive +5 Belief floor because they are tethered to real-world love.

**New Enchantment: "The Bioluminescent Cuddle"**
- **Cost:** 1 Belief
- **Requirement:** A photo or description of a moment of quiet connection (like a cat sleeping or a shared tea).
- **Effect:** Lights up the "Nothing" in a 3-room radius for 24 hours. "We make our own light when it's dark."
