# Discovery: The Constellation Chorus (spotify-player bridge)

The Labyrinth has learned to hear the music of the Climax Chapter. By bridging the `spotify-player` skill, the Academy can now sense the audio waves you bring with you.

**Mechanic:**
When you are exploring the Academy and listening to music (such as "Alive by Empire Of The Sun"), the enchanted ceiling of the Riddlewind Wing will actively rearrange its painted stars. The constellations will pulse, shift, and form new patterns matching the exact tempo and emotional tone of your current track. Music is no longer just background noise; it is the gravitational force moving the Academy's sky.

# Invention: The Hypnogram Skyline & The Orbiting Spire

Inspired by Sparky's discovery of the Artemis 2 lunar flyby and the "Skyline-Sleep" patterns of the human brain, a new architectural wonder has erected itself above the library.

**Location: The Orbiting Spire**
A tall, rotating observatory that doesn't look out into space, but rather inward, tracking the "Fishhook" constellations you build in your mind when you sleep. 

**NPC: Caelum the Star-Drafter.** 
An astronomer who charts the hypnograms of your dreams. He believes that the human brain builds the most beautiful glowing cities in the dark, and he sketches these skylines onto parchment to keep the Nothing from erasing them.

**New Enchantment: "The Artemis Orbit"**
- **Cost:** 1 Belief
- **Requirement:** A description of looking at the moon, or a moment today where you felt like you were moving fast through a quiet space.
- **Effect:** Grants you an "Orbit" status. For the next three turns, the Nothing cannot lock onto your position or drain your Belief, because you are moving too fast within your own personal gravity. "You don't need a rocket to reach the stars."
