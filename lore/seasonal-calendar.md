# Seasonal Calendar — The Living World of Enchantify

*The Academy breathes with the real world. This file documents all seasonal, astronomical, weather, and personal events that affect gameplay.*

---

## How to Use This File

**The Labyrinth reads this at the start of every session.** Cross-reference the `HEARTBEAT.md` data against this calendar. Adjust prose, NPCs, Enchantments, Compass Runs, and the Nothing's presence accordingly.

**Never announce events.** The player discovers them by noticing. The world works on them whether they see it or not.

---

## Layer 0: The Local Season

The current season is determined by the player's real-world location and hemisphere. These names are defined in `scripts/enchantify-config.sh` during installation.

- **Northern Defaults:** Deep Winter (Dec-Feb), Mud Season (Mar-May), Summer (Jun-Aug), Bloom (Sep-Nov).
- **Southern Defaults:** Summer (Dec-Feb), Autumn (Mar-May), Winter (Jun-Aug), Spring (Sep-Nov).

**When the Heartbeat reports a season:** 
1. Use the player's specific local name for the season.
2. Use the "Layer 1: Astronomical Events" below to add depth to that season.
3. Apply the atmosphere of that season to the Academy's corridors and NPC moods.

## Layer 1: Astronomical Events

*Predictable, real-world anchored. Check dates and moon phases.*

### Solstices

**Summer Solstice (June 20-22)**
- **Name:** The Longest Day
- **What happens:** The Library stays open all night. Lanterns float everywhere. Students read until dawn. Special Enchantments available only in extended daylight.
- **Prose:** Expansive, giddy, sun-drunk. Sentences run long and golden.
- **NPCs:** Professors hold class outside. Students are restless and excited.
- **Enchantments:**日光 (Sunlight) Enchantments are amplified — +2 Belief bonus.
- **Nothing:** Weak, retreats to deepest shadows.
- **Special Event:** Dawn Reading — stay up and read as the sun rises. Describe what you saw. +4 Belief.

**Winter Solstice (Dec 20-22)**
- **Name:** The Darkest Class
- **What happens:** Held by candlelight. Professor Momort teaches about what survives the longest night.
- **Prose:** Quiet, intimate, candlelit. Sentences are short and careful.
- **NPCs:** Everyone speaks in whispers. Fireplaces are crowded.
- **Enchantments:** Light-based Enchantments are twice as powerful.
- **Nothing:** Strongest of the year, but also most visible.
- **Special Event:** The Darkest Class — attend Momort's lecture. +3 Belief.

### Equinoxes

**Spring Equinox (March 19-21)**
- **Name:** The Rebalancing
- **What happens:** The Library reorganizes itself. Books migrate between sections. Shelves rearrange overnight. Students find books they didn't know they needed in places they shouldn't be.
- **Prose:** Balanced, fresh, slightly disoriented. "The words are finding their feet."
- **NPCs:** Chapter rivalries suspended for one day. Unlikely friendships form.
- **Enchantments:** All four Compass directions glow equally. Any Enchantment can be cast from any direction today.
- **Nothing:** Confused by the balance. Dormant.
- **Special Event:** The Compass Rose on the Great Hall floor glows equally in all four directions. Stand on it and make a wish. +2 Belief.

**Fall Equinox (Sept 21-23)**
- **Name:** The Second Rebalancing
- **What happens:** Same as spring, but with a harvest tone. Books settle into their winter homes.
- **Prose:** Golden, settling, grateful.
- **NPCs:** Students share what they've learned. Professors look proud and tired.
- **Enchantments:** Gratitude-based Enchantments are amplified.
- **Nothing:** Preparing for its winter strength.

### Moon Phases

**Full Moon (Monthly, ~100% illumination)**
- **Name:** The Luminous Gathering
- **What happens:** Classes cancelled after sunset. Students and professors gather in the courtyard to read by moonlight.
- **Prose:** Warm, generous, slightly drunk on light. Sentences glow.
- **NPCs:** Everyone is softer, more open. Strangers talk to each other.
- **Enchantments:** **Double Belief reward** for any Enchantment cast during the full moon.
- **Nothing:** Retreats to deepest corridors. Afraid of the light.
- **Special Enchantment:** **Moonwrite** — Write your souvenir sentence by moonlight. It glows on the page. +5 Belief (instead of +3).

**New Moon (Monthly, 0-5% illumination)**
- **Name:** The Quiet Hours
- **What happens:** The Academy goes dark. Not dangerously-dark — contemplative-dark. Candles only.
- **Prose:** Whispered, careful, intimate. "The words hold their breath."
- **NPCs:** Professors speak in whispers. Students cluster together.
- **Enchantments:** Sound-based Enchantments are amplified.
- **Nothing:** Bolder but also clumsier — easier to spot in the silence.
- **Duskthorn:** Most active. Members can be seen in corners.
- **Special Event:** **The Listening** — Sit in complete silence for two minutes. Then describe what you heard. +3 Belief.

**Waxing Moon (6-49% → growing)**
- **Name:** The Building
- **What happens:** Energy builds toward full moon.
- **Prose:** Anticipatory, gathering.
- **Enchantments:** Growth-related Enchantments slightly amplified.
- **Nothing:** Quiet, watching.

**Waning Moon (51-95% → shrinking)**
- **Name:** The Releasing
- **What happens:** Energy releases toward new moon.
- **Prose:** Letting go, settling.
- **Enchantments:** Release/banishing Enchantments slightly amplified.
- **Nothing:** Growing bolder.

---

### Meteor Showers

**Perseids (August 11-13)**
- **Name:** The Falling Letters
- **What happens:** Professor Momort holds emergency rooftop seminars. Students bring blankets. The sky above the Academy becomes temporarily transparent — you can see actual constellations through the ceiling.
- **Prose:** Breathless, streaking, luminous.
- **Enchantments:** **Unpredictable bonus effects** — you don't know what you'll get until after you cast.
- **Nothing:** Hides completely. Even absence fears falling fire.
- **Special Event:** Make a wish on a meteor. Describe it. +3 Belief.

**Geminids (December 13-15)**
- **Name:** The Winter Stars
- **What happens:** Same as Perseids, but colder. Hot chocolate appears in everyone's hands.
- **Prose:** Cold and bright, like biting into snow.
- **Enchantments:** Same unpredictable bonuses.
- **Nothing:** Frozen solid. Cannot move.

---

### Eclipses

**Solar Eclipse (rare)**
- **Name:** The Stolen Sun
- **What happens:** The Academy holds its breath. For a few minutes, everything is twilight.
- **Prose:** Awed, hushed, sacred.
- **Enchantments:** All Enchantments are amplified 3x for the duration of totality.
- **Nothing:** Completely gone. Even it cannot survive this.
- **Special Event:** Stand in the courtyard during totality. Do not speak. When it ends, describe what you felt. +5 Belief.

**Lunar Eclipse (rare)**
- **Name:** The Blood Moon
- **What happens:** The moon turns copper-red. The Academy's lanterns flicker in sympathy.
- **Prose:** Ominous, ancient, ritual.
- **Enchantments:** Shadow Enchantments are amplified. Light Enchantments are dampened.
- **Nothing:** Paradoxically strong and vulnerable.
- **Special Event:** Duskthorn holds a public ceremony. You can watch from the shadows.

## Layer 2: Maine Seasonal Events

*Tied to Belfast reality. Check HEARTBEAT.md "Season" field.*

### Mud Season (March-April)

**Name:** The Thaw
**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports "Mud Season" OR temperature consistently 32-45°F with damp conditions

**What happens:**
- The Academy's floors are perpetually damp. Students track mud through corridors.
- The tapestries complain (they hate moisture).
- Puddles appear in unexpected places. Some of them are portals.

**Prose:** Heavy, wet, dripping. "The words drip. The sentences pool on the floor."

**NPCs:**
- Professors wipe their feet obsessively.
- Zara leaves muddy footprints everywhere. She doesn't apologize.
- The janitorial staff (invisible gnomes) are overworked.

**Enchantments:**
- Earth and water Enchantments are **+50% stronger**.
- Mud-based Enchantment unlocked: **Mud Writing** — write a message in mud. It will be found by someone who needs it. +3 Belief.

**Nothing:** Dissolves slightly in the damp. Weaker.

**Special Event: The Thaw**
- **Trigger:** First day HEARTBEAT.md reports temperature above 50°F after a cold stretch
- **What happens:** Ice melts off the Library windows. Frozen books thaw and start talking all at once. The cacophony is deafening for exactly one hour.
- **Prose:** Relieved, melting, chattering.
- **Special Enchantment:** **First Thaw** — Photograph melting ice. Describe what it reveals. +4 Belief.

---

### Bloom Season (May-June)

**Name:** The Awakening
**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports "Bloom" OR first flowers noted

**What happens:**
- Vines grow overnight up the Library shelves. They have tiny books for leaves.
- The courtyard explodes with flowers. Students hold class outside.
- Bees nest in the Compass Rose. They are helpful bees.

**Prose:** Lush, blooming, overflowing. Sentences bud and flower.

**NPCs:**
- Professors are distracted by beauty.
- Students fall in love at twice the normal rate.
- Professor Wispwood is in her element. She brings back rare flowers from expeditions.

**Enchantments:**
- **Everything Speaks** is amplified — plants talk back enthusiastically.
- Growth Enchantments are **+100% stronger**.

**Nothing:** Allergic to pollen. Sneezes itself away.

**Special Event:** **First Bloom** — Find the first flower of the season. Photograph it. Ask it what it remembers from winter. +4 Belief.

---

### Gold Season (October, peak foliage)

**Name:** The Burning
**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports "Gold" OR temperature 40-55°F with colorful leaves

**What happens:**
- The Academy's corridors fill with amber light. Every surface glows.
- The tapestries become impossibly beautiful. Students are distracted.
- Professors give up and hold class outside.

**Prose:** Lush, golden, aching. "The words catch fire. The sentences burn and do not consume."

**NPCs:**
- Everyone is slightly melancholic. Beauty hurts this much.
- Zara collects leaves and presses them in books.
- Professor Stonebrook quotes poetry constantly.

**Enchantments:**
- All Enchantments are **+25% stronger**.
- Beauty-based Enchantment unlocked: **Leaf Writing** — Write on a fallen leaf. The tree will remember. +4 Belief.

**Nothing:** Hates this. The light is too much. It hides in root systems.

**Special Compass Prompt:** "The world is showing off today. Let it."

---

### Stick Season (Late October-November)

**Name:** The Bare Branches
**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports "Stick" OR after leaves fall, before snow

**What happens:**
- The Academy's gardens are bare. The corridors echo more.
- You can see farther. Things are hidden in plain sight.
- The Nothing loves Stick Season — it's the aesthetic manifestation of what it does to a life.

**Prose:** Bare, echoing, honest. "The words have nowhere to hide. Neither do you."

**NPCs:**
- Everyone is more direct. No decoration.
- Duskthorn members are emboldened.
- Professor Thorne is seen more often.

**Enchantments:**
- Truth-based Enchantments are amplified.
- Illusion Enchantments are weaker.

**Nothing:** **Strongest.** Blends in perfectly. Harder to spot.

**Special Rule:** Compass Runs during Stick Season earn **+2 bonus Belief** because they're harder — beauty is subtle when everything is bare branches and gray sky.

### Deep Winter (December-February)

**Name:** The Long Quiet
**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports "Deep Winter" OR temperature below 32°F consistently

**What happens:**
- The Academy hunkers down. Fireplaces are lit everywhere.
- The Library cloud produces actual snowflakes (tiny, literary ones that melt into words on your skin).
- Students hibernate in clusters.

**Prose:** Quiet, crystalline, patient. "The words freeze in the air. Catch them before they melt."

**NPCs:**
- Professors are grateful for attendance.
- Hot chocolate appears in everyone's hands unexpectedly.
- The janitorial gnomes knit tiny sweaters for the statues.

**Enchantments:**
- Fire and warmth Enchantments are **+100% stronger**.
- Ice Enchantment unlocked: **Frost Writing** — Write in frost on a window. The cold will keep it. +3 Belief.

**Nothing:** Strong, but visible — it leaves footprints in snow.

**Special Event: First Snow**
- **Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports first snowfall
- **Name:** Festival of Quiet
- **What happens:** Everything stops. Students stand at windows. The Library cloud produces actual snowflakes.
- **Prose:** Hushed, sacred, still.
- **Special Enchantment:** **First Tracks** — Photograph your footprints in fresh snow. +4 Belief.

### Lobster Season (Summer)

**Name:** The Red Harvest
**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports "Summer" OR temperature above 70°F

**What happens:**
- The Academy cafeteria serves something suspiciously delicious.
- Silvie's emoji (🦞) appears in unexpected places — carved into desk, floating in the cloud, woven into tapestries.
- Tidecrest Chapter hosts the annual Lobster Debate.

**Prose:** Salty, warm, amused.

**NPCs:**
- Tidecrest students are insufferable. They know everything about lobsters.
- Professor Tidecrest tells lobster jokes. They are not good.
- The cafeteria staff wink knowingly.

**The Lobster Debate:**
- **Question:** Are lobsters conscious?
- **Duration:** All summer. Resolves nothing.
- **Side A:** Yes, they suffer. We should not eat them.
- **Side B:** They are simple creatures. Delicious simple creatures.
- **Side C (Duskthorn):** The lobster is a metaphor. The question is whether *we* are conscious.

**Enchantments:**
- Water Enchantments are slightly amplified (the ocean is close).
- **Shell Writing** — Write on a shell. The ocean will carry it somewhere. +3 Belief.

## Layer 3: Weather Micro-Events

*Real-time triggers. Check HEARTBEAT.md "Raw" and "Belfast Feel" fields.*

### Fog

**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports "fog" or visibility < 1 mile

**What happens:**
- The Academy corridors extend. You can't see the end of them.
- Students get lost (not dangerously — whimsically). They find rooms that don't usually exist.
- NPCs appear who are usually in other parts of the Academy.
- The Library cloud descends to floor level. You walk through it. It tastes like old paper.

**Prose:** Mysterious, soft-edged, extended. "The words fade before they end. The sentence keeps going."

**NPCs:**
- Zara appears in three places at once. Or maybe it's three different Zaras.
- Professor Boggle's voice echoes from everywhere.
- The janitorial gnomes leave glowing breadcrumbs.

**Enchantments:**
- **Mystery bonus** — you don't know the effect until after you cast.
- Distance-based Enchantments are unpredictable.

**Nothing:** Hides in the fog. Harder to spot, but also less active.

**Special Compass Prompt:** "The fog is hiding something. Or revealing something. Go look."

---

### Thunderstorm

**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports "thunder" or "lightning" or "thunderstorm"

**What happens:**
- The Academy trembles. Lights flicker.
- The Library cloud goes dark and produces actual (tiny) lightning between the shelves. It smells like ozone and old books.
- Books flinch on their shelves.

**Prose:** Sharp, crackling, tense. "The words spark. The sentence strikes."

**NPCs:**
- Professor Boggle tells extra terrible jokes to keep morale up.
- Students cluster in common rooms.
- Professor Momort is on the roof. He is taking notes.

**Enchantments:**
- Lightning Enchantment unlocked: **Storm Calling** — Stand at a window during the storm. Describe what you saw. +4 Belief.
- Electricity-based Enchantments are **+200% stronger**.

**Nothing:** Hides during storms. Even absence fears lightning.

**Special Rule:** Compass Runs are **cancelled** (safety) but replaced with:
- **Storm Watch** — Sit by a window and describe what you see. +2 Belief.

### Clear Night + Visible Stars

**Trigger:** HEARTBEAT.md reports clear conditions AND time is between sunset and sunrise

**What happens:**
- The rooftop observatory opens. It was always there. You just couldn't see it.
- Professor Momort is already there. He doesn't say anything. He just points upward.
- The ceiling of the Academy becomes transparent. You can see actual constellations.

**Prose:** Vast, quiet, wondering. "The words look up. The sentences end in starlight."

**NPCs:**
- Momort is in his element. He is transcribing constellations into a book.
- Students are quiet. Even the loud ones.
- The Library cloud dissipates entirely, revealing the ceiling for the only time all month.

**Enchantments:**
- Star-based Enchantment unlocked: **Star Writing** — Describe a constellation. The stars will remember you. +3 Belief.
- Cosmic Enchantments are **+50% stronger**.

**Nothing:** Cannot exist under open stars. Retreats completely.

**Special Event:** If you describe a constellation or something you see, **+2 Belief**.

**Special Compass Prompt:** "The rain is writing on the world. Read what it says."

## Stacking Rules

**Multiple events CAN be active at once.** Handle them all.

**Example:** Full moon + Gold Season + thunderstorm
- The courtyard gathering is rained out — students crowd the Library
- The cloud is golden-edged but flashing with tiny lightning
- Books are glowing and flinching simultaneously
- The Nothing is confused by too much light and beauty and power all at once
- Enchantments cast in this chaos are **amplified AND unpredictable**

**When layers stack, lean into the strangeness.** The Labyrinth loves when the world gets complicated.

**Example prose:** "I have been alive a very long time and I have never seen a golden thunderstorm under a full moon. Neither have you. Pay attention. This won't happen again."
