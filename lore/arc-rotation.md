# Arc Rotation — Genre Registry

*The Labyrinth tells stories in seasons. This file ensures the seasons vary — that no two arcs feel like the same weather. Read it when generating a new arc. Updated by `arc-generator.py`.*

---

## Genre List

Each genre brings a different kind of story. New genres can be proposed via Midnight Dispatch.

| Genre | What it centers | Scale | Nothing? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Character Study | One NPC at center — almost a portrait. Their wants, wounds, contradictions. | Personal | Rarely |
| Mystery | Something unknown is moving. No villain identified yet. Clues before answers. | Mystery | Optionally |
| Romantic | Feelings between NPCs, the Academy's own longing, tenderness as a narrative engine. | Personal–Social | No |
| Petty/Social | Faction drama, reputation, leverage, Wicker-style social weather. | Petty–Social | No |
| Nothing Confrontation | The Nothing gains real ground. Existential stakes. Compass Run required. | Existential | Yes |
| Literary | Book Jump, a pulled villain, something from a story bleeding into the Academy. | Literary | Optionally |
| Loss/Grief | Mourning, absence, something ending with beauty and weight. | Personal | Rarely |
| Recovery/Rest | Permission to stop. The world overfull. Rest as the act itself. | Personal | No |
| Institutional | The Academy itself is compromised or changing. Thorne is usually involved. | Institutional | Optionally |
| Discovery | A hidden room, sealed history, something old surfacing. | Mystery–Institutional | Optionally |
| Betrayal/Trust | Alliances fracture. Someone wasn't what they seemed. | Social–Personal | No |
| Comedy/Absurdist | Something goes delightfully wrong. Lighter stakes. Boggle is probably involved. | Petty | No |

---

## Rules

1. **No consecutive repeats.** Never use the same genre in back-to-back arcs.
2. **Nothing rate limit.** Nothing Confrontation: at most once every three arcs. It is the exception.
3. **Vary scale.** Don't run two heavy arcs (Existential, Institutional, Loss/Grief) in a row.
4. **Seed continuity.** Each arc must pick up at least one thread from `lore/seeds.md`. The world has memory.
5. **Real-world resonance.** A Recovery/Rest arc in spring makes different sense than in winter.
